About me

I'm Pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Cybersecurity. Since July 2022, I have been a third-year undergraduate, and in parallel, I have been actively involved in bug bounty programs since March 2024. My dedication to uncovering and reporting vulnerabilities aims to enhance the security of various platforms. My journey in cybersecurity has been both challenging and rewarding. In March 2024, I earned my first Hall of Fame recognition for reporting a significant bug in the Achmea Vulnerability Disclosure Program, marking the beginning of my bug bounty career. As of July 2024, I have accumulated valuable experience and a total of $700 in bounty rewards. specialize in identifying vulnerabilities and contributing to the security of organizations through detailed and effective reports. My goal is to continuously improve my skills, stay updated with the latest in cybersecurity, and make meaningful contributions to the field. When I'm not hunting for bugs, I enjoy reading articles related to cybersecurity, studying vulnerability reports, and engaging with the cybersecurity community to learn and share knowledge.
